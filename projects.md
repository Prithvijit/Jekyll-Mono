### [Counting Everyday Objects in Everyday Scenes](https://arxiv.org/abs/1604.03505)
**Prithvijit Chattopadhyay**, **Ramakrishna Vedantam**, **Ramprasaath Selvaraju**, **Devi Parikh**, **Dhruv Batra** 
<br/>
We introduce the problem of counting everyday objects in everyday scenes. While previous works have studied specific counting problems such as pedestrian counting in surveillance videos, or biological cell counting, we are interested in counting common objects in natural scenes. Given an image, we consider the task of predicting the counts (or the numerosity) of categories of interest. We study some simple approaches and applications for this counting problem.
